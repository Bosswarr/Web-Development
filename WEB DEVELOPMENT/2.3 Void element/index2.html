My top 3 Movies all of the time

Hero Academia

Hero Academia is a Japanese superhero manga series written and illustrated by Katsuki Bakugo. The story follows the adventures of a young boy named Izuku Midoriya, who dreams of becoming a hero despite being born without superpowers.

Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. The story follows the adventures of Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons.
